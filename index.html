Cultural Highlights

Why American Culture Matters

American culture represents a unique blend of traditions from around the world, creating a dynamic and ever-evolving tapestry of art, food, music, and ideas.

From the Native American heritage to the influences of immigrants from every corner of the globe, the United States has developed a culture that both honors tradition and embraces innovation.
